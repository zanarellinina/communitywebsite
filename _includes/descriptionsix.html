Project Overview

Telling a story through a photograph collection is not easy but it is the only way to describe oneself on a global level, it is the only tool that does not encounter language or educational obstacles. Through this small photographic collection, I have tried to represent a part of the most important experience of my life and how it has been totally changed because of the beginning of the covid-19 pandemic. The aim of the project is therefore to transmit a part of my experience to the viewer, trying to make them understand the emotions I felt during that period.
